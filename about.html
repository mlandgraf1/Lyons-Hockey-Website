About Us

The St. Louis Lyons are a women's hockey team that play in the St. Louis Area. We've brought together players of all ages and skill levels to create a competitive, yet fun hockey experience. We play annually in the Women's Midwestern Hockey League, as well playing in tournaments around the Midwest. We encourage anyone interested in playing with us, even those with no experience, to contact us to see what we're all about!

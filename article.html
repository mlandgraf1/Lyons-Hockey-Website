Lyons Defeat Wasps

In a thrilling showdown between the St. Louis Lyons and the Chicago Wasps, the Lyons mounted an incredible come from behind victory to advance to the semifinals in the 2018 Women's Hockey Fights Cancer Tournament.

The Wasps came out flying early, knocking a rebound past the Lyons' goalie to take a 1-0, which was followed a few minutes later by a shot from the point that was deflected into the top left corner to make the score 2-0 for the Wasps. However, the Lyons gained momentum early in the third period by capitalizing on a powerplay opportunity to make the score 2-1. With two minutes remaining in the period, the Lyons pulled their goalie for the extra skater, who intercepted a stretch pass by the Wasps and sniped a goal from the high slot to tie the game. The Lyons then pinned the Wasps into their own zone for the last minute of the third period, and a slap shot from the point found its way past the Wasp's goalie to seal the win for Lyons with a score of 3-2.
